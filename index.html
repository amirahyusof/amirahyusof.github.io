About Me

As a committed stay-at-home mother and an enthusiastic front-end developer, I have always placed a high value on continuing to learn new things. I started my path of self-teaching to become a front-end developer while running the house and raising my kids. It has been a rewarding and difficult experience, and I can't wait to use my newly acquired knowledge in a work environment.

My expertise is in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which I diligently improved over many hours of work and commitment. I find the field of web design to be really fascinating, and I enjoy the opportunities it presents for developing visually appealing and captivating user interfaces. In addition, my passion for lifelong learning has inspired me to look deeper into the React framework, which has expanded my knowledge base and given me the tools I need to create dynamic, interactive online applications.

I am driven to establish my worth and contribute significantly to a company that appreciates ingenuity, inventiveness, and resourcefulness.
